Who We Are

At Cabinet Yachfin, led by Dr. Sehtal Narimane, we are committed to providing advanced and reliable dental care to the community of Batna. Our clinic combines modern technology, strict hygiene standards, and patient-centered practices to ensure exceptional results in every treatment. We focus on delivering high-quality dental services, including preventive care, restorative procedures, cosmetic dentistry, and oral surgery, all with the goal of improving both your oral health and your confidence. At Yachfin, every patient receives personalized attention in a comfortable and professional environment.

Our best-in-class dental innovation, and in particular our proceeding with an expert training program for every one of the individuals from our dental group has a noteworthy influence in achieving that objective.
